Privacy Policy

Third Sleep Tracker

Last Updated: January 2025
Our Privacy Promise: Your sleep data belongs to you. We store everything locally on your device. No account required, no cloud sync, no tracking. Your data never leaves your phone unless you explicitly choose to contribute to research.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Third ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal information.

By using the Third app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Data Stored Locally on Your Device

All of the following data is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's Core Data framework. This data never leaves your phone unless you opt in to research data sharing (see Section 3):

  • Sleep Quality Data: Your daily sleep scores, sleep duration, time to fall asleep, and wake method
  • Lifestyle Factors: Caffeine intake and timing, exercise intensity and timing, alcohol consumption, stress levels, hydration levels, and meal timing
  • Personal Information: Age range (not exact age), gender (optional), target bedtime, and target wake time
  • App Usage Data: Check-in completion status, tracking streaks, challenge participation and progress, and insights calculation results
  • Preferences: Notification settings, tracking preferences (which factors you want to track), and theme preferences
Important: This data is stored using Apple's secure Core Data storage. It is not accessible to other apps, is backed up to iCloud if you have iCloud backup enabled for apps, and is deleted when you delete the app.

2.2 Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or have access to:

  • Your name, email address, or phone number (no account required)
  • Your exact location or GPS data
  • Your device's unique identifier (unless you register a device for development)
  • Your contacts, photos, or other device data
  • Your browsing history or data from other apps
  • Any financial or payment information

3. Optional Research Data Sharing

During onboarding and in Settings, you have the option to contribute anonymous, de-identified sleep data to help improve sleep research. This is completely optional and opt-in.

3.1 What Data is Shared (If You Opt In)

If you consent to research data sharing, we collect and upload the following anonymized data:

Data Type What's Included
Sleep Patterns Sleep quality scores, duration, time to fall asleep (anonymized)
Factor Correlations Statistical relationships between caffeine, exercise, stress, etc. and sleep quality
Demographics Age range (e.g., "25-34"), gender (if provided), general timezone
Challenge Data Which challenges were attempted and completed (anonymized)

3.2 What is NOT Shared (Even If You Opt In)

We never share:
  • Your name or any personally identifiable information
  • Exact dates or timestamps (we use relative days only)
  • Your exact location or device information
  • Any data that could be used to identify you
  • Notes or free-text entries (if we add this feature in the future)

3.3 How We Anonymize Your Data

When you opt in to research sharing, we:

  1. Assign you a random, unique research ID (not linked to any personal information)
  2. Remove all exact dates and replace with relative day numbers (e.g., "Day 1", "Day 2")
  3. Replace exact ages with age ranges (e.g., "25-34")
  4. Remove device identifiers and IP addresses
  5. Aggregate data with other users before analysis

3.4 Where Research Data is Stored

Anonymized research data is stored on Supabase, a secure, privacy-focused database platform. The data is:

  • Encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Protected by Row Level Security (RLS) policies
  • Accessible only through anonymous API keys
  • Not sold or shared with third parties
  • Used solely for sleep research and app improvement

3.5 Opting Out of Research Data Sharing

You can enable or disable research data sharing at any time:

  1. Open the Third app
  2. Go to Settings → Data & Privacy
  3. Toggle "Share anonymous data for research"

Note: Opting out stops future uploads but does not delete previously uploaded anonymous data (as it cannot be linked back to you).

4. How We Use Your Information

4.1 Data Stored Locally

The data stored on your device is used to:

  • Calculate personalized sleep insights and correlations
  • Track your progress and streaks
  • Generate challenges based on your data
  • Display charts and visualizations
  • Send you notification reminders (if enabled)
  • Improve your sleep quality through data-driven insights

4.2 Anonymous Research Data (If Shared)

Anonymized research data is used to:

  • Improve Third's insights algorithms for all users
  • Contribute to sleep science research
  • Understand population-level sleep patterns
  • Develop better sleep tracking features
  • Publish aggregate findings in scientific research (no individual data)

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

5.1 We Do Not Sell Your Data

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

5.2 Third-Party Services We Use

The app uses the following third-party services:

Service Purpose Data Accessed
Apple Push Notification Service (APNs) Send daily reminder notifications Device token (managed by Apple, not us)
Supabase Store anonymous research data (opt-in only) Anonymized sleep data (see Section 3)
Apple Core Data Local data storage on your device All app data (stored locally only)

5.3 No Analytics or Tracking SDKs

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs such as:

  • Google Analytics
  • Facebook SDK
  • Advertising networks
  • Crash reporting services
  • A/B testing platforms

6. Data Security

We take data security seriously and implement industry-standard measures:

6.1 Local Data Protection

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted using Apple's secure Core Data storage
  • Device Lock: Data is protected by your device's passcode/biometrics
  • Sandboxing: App data is isolated from other apps
  • Secure Backups: iCloud backups (if enabled) are encrypted

6.2 Research Data Protection

  • HTTPS/TLS: All data transmissions are encrypted in transit
  • Row Level Security: Database access is restricted by security policies
  • Anonymous Keys: API keys have minimal permissions (write-only for user data)
  • No PII Storage: No personally identifiable information is stored on servers

6.3 Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach involving anonymized research data, we will:

  1. Investigate the breach immediately
  2. Notify affected users via app update and email (if we have it)
  3. Report to relevant authorities as required by law
  4. Take steps to prevent future breaches

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Access Your Data

All your data is stored locally on your device and is accessible anytime through the app. You can view your complete history in Settings → Edit Sleep Data.

7.2 Edit or Delete Your Data

  • Edit entries: Settings → Edit Sleep Data → Select any day to modify
  • Delete specific entries: Mark days as "Ignore this sleep" to exclude from insights
  • Delete all data: Settings → Pause or Reset Tracking → Delete All Data

7.3 Export Your Data

Coming soon: We're working on a data export feature that will let you download your complete sleep history in CSV format.

7.4 Opt Out of Research Sharing

Settings → Data & Privacy → Toggle "Share anonymous data for research" off

7.5 Disable Notifications

You can disable notifications at any time:

  • In the app: Settings → Notifications → Toggle off morning/evening reminders
  • iOS Settings: Settings → Third → Notifications → Turn off

8. Children's Privacy

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at thirdsleepapp@gmail.com and we will delete such information.

Age Requirement: You must be at least 13 years old to use Third. If you are between 13 and 18, please use the app with parental consent.

9. International Users

Third is available worldwide. If you are using the app outside of the United States:

  • Your data is stored locally on your device (no international transfer)
  • If you opt in to research sharing, anonymous data may be stored on servers in the United States
  • We comply with applicable data protection laws including GDPR (EU) and CCPA (California)

9.1 GDPR Rights (EU Users)

If you are in the European Union, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data (stored locally on your device)
  • Rectify inaccurate data (edit in the app)
  • Erase your data ("right to be forgotten" - delete the app)
  • Restrict processing (disable research sharing)
  • Data portability (export feature coming soon)
  • Object to processing (opt out of research sharing)

9.2 CCPA Rights (California Users)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect (see Section 2)
  • Delete your personal information (delete the app or specific entries)
  • Opt out of the "sale" of personal information (we don't sell data)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your rights

11. Data Retention

11.1 Local Data

Data stored on your device is retained indefinitely until you:

  • Delete the app (permanently removes all data)
  • Use the "Delete All Data" feature in Settings
  • Delete individual entries

11.2 Research Data

Anonymous research data (if you opted in) is retained for:

  • Active research purposes (minimum 2 years)
  • Long-term sleep science studies (up to 10 years)
  • Aggregate analysis and published research (indefinitely, but anonymized)

Because research data is fully anonymized, we cannot identify or delete your specific contributions if you request deletion.
